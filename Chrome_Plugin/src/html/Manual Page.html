Product Manual

Developer: Anthony Leung, Addision Kuykendall, Benny Li, Jinheng Zhang, Yisi Yin

Product BackGround:

In an environment where fake news has become a major social issue and a technical challenge for social media companies to identify fake news. This product classifies and distinguishes satire from legitimate news by collecting data on users' comments on news reports.

User Instructions:

1. Open up your Chrome browser and type "chrome://extensions/". 2. Loads the extracted chrome extension.
3. Enter any ID you want firstly.
4. Comment on the current content of your web page.
5. You need to include the reasons for this evaluation.
6. After clicking the Submit button, you will see your data in our Firebase Database.
